SOOT is a new way of mapping information. It's self-organizing, spatial, and incredibly fast. For mapping design files, videos, websites, and moodboards.

SOOT has been featured in DAZED Magazine. See interviews powered by SOOT including STUDIOCULT (Explore space), Fatzoo (Explore space), and Paul Nicholson (Explore space).
